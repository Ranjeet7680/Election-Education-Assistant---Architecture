---

👤 Priya: Register as voter

🤖 Assistant: 📝 **Voter Registration Guide**

To register as a voter, follow these simple steps:

**Step 1: Check Eligibility**
✓ You must be 18 years or older
✓ You must be an Indian citizen
✓ You should be a resident of the constituency where you want to register

**Step 2: Gather Required Documents**
• Age Proof (Required)
• Address Proof (Required)
• Recent Photograph (Required)
• Aadhaar Card (Optional)

**Step 3: Apply Online**
Visit: https://voters.eci.gov.in/
Or download the Voter Helpline App

Submit your application and track its status online

Call helpline: 1950

Would you like me to explain any specific step in detail?

👤 Priya: What documents do I need?

For Registration:
📄 **Age Proof** (Required)
  Document proving you are 18 years or older

📄 **Address Proof** (Required)
  Document proving your current residential address
  Alternatives: Aadhaar, Passport, Utility Bill, Rent Agreement

  Passport-size photograph (not older than 3 months)

📄 **Aadhaar Card** (Optional)
  Unique identification number issued by UIDAI
  Alternatives: Passport, Driving License, PAN Card

For Voting:
You need to carry **any ONE** valid photo ID:
• Voter ID (EPIC)
• Aadhaar Card
• Passport
• Driving License
• PAN Card
• Service ID (for government employees)

⚠️ Important: Carry original documents, not photocopies!

---

👤 Lakshmi: What is EVM and VVPAT?

🤖 Assistant: 🖥️ **Understanding EVM and VVPAT**

**EVM (Electronic Voting Machine)**
• Electronic device used for casting votes
• Replaced paper ballots in India
• Has two units:
  1. Control Unit (with polling officer)
  2. Balloting Unit (where you press button)

**How it works:**
1. Polling officer activates the machine
2. You see list of candidates with symbols
3. You press button next to your choice
4. Machine beeps and shows red light
5. Your vote is recorded

**Benefits:**
✓ Fast and accurate
✓ No invalid votes
✓ Easy to use
✓ Eco-friendly (no paper waste)

---

**VVPAT (Voter Verifiable Paper Audit Trail)**
• Attached to EVM
• Prints a paper slip when you vote
• Shows whom you voted for
• Visible for 7 seconds through a glass window
• Then automatically cuts and drops into sealed box

**Why VVPAT?**
✓ Transparency - You can verify your vote
✓ Audit trail - Can be used for verification if needed
✓ Builds trust in the system

**Important:**
⚠️ You cannot take the VVPAT slip with you
⚠️ It automatically drops into a sealed box
⚠️ Just verify it shows your choice correctly

💡 **Fun Fact**: India is one of the first countries to use VVPAT 
nationwide!

2. **Verification & Voter List Publication** ⏳ UPCOMING

5. **Voting Day** ⏳ UPCOMING

• Attend public meetings if interested
• Listen to candidates' speeches
• Ask questions if there's Q&A

**4. Verify Information** ✓
• Check facts from multiple sources
• Don't believe everything on social media
• Use official sources for verification

**5. Discuss with Others** 💬
• Talk to family and friends
• Understand different perspectives
• Make an informed decision

**6. Stay Updated** 📰
• Follow news from reliable sources
• Watch candidate debates
• Check official election commission updates

**What NOT to do:**
❌ Don't accept money or gifts for votes
❌ Don't spread fake news
❌ Don't engage in violence or hate speech
❌ Don't vote based on caste, religion, or money

**Remember:**
